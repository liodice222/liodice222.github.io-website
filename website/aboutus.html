Lea

hi! I have been a personal trainer since 2017- my main interests are finding anatomical alignment and making your exercise routine enjoyable. I also completed my 200 hour Yoga Teacher Training focusing on non-performative yoga. I enjoy exploring the interconnections between our emotional body and physical body and holding space for people to find freedom of expression through their yoga practice.



Abbey

Hey there :) My name is Abbey, and I am a registered yoga instructor based in the Baltimore/DC area. I completed my 200hr training at Highland Yoga in Atlanta, GA in the fall of 2021. I am also a board certified health coach and work in clinical nutrition research! Yoga has transformed my being and soul, helping me to stay present. I believe the community that surrounds yoga is truly a unique one, having seen how it can transform lives. I wish to guide others on their yoga journey, whether they are just starting out or have years of experience.
